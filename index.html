About Us

Last Hope Foundation is a youth-led organization dedicated to improving the lives of children, families, and people with disabilities in Liberia through education, advocacy, and community programs.

Our Area

We focus on humanitarian support, youth empowerment, education, and community development to restore hope and dignity.

Our Mission

To restore hope and improve the quality of life for vulnerable individuals and communities through compassion-driven humanitarian service, advocacy, and sustainable support.

Founder & Executive Director

James K. Dargbeh

Founder & Executive Director, Last Hope Foundation

James K. Dargbeh is a passionate humanitarian, youth leader, and advocate for vulnerable communities in Liberia. Driven by faith, compassion, and firsthand exposure to the struggles of marginalized people, he founded the Last Hope Foundation to restore hope and create meaningful change.

Beyond humanitarian work, James has demonstrated strong leadership and academic excellence. He served as Assistant Captain of the Liberia National High School Quizzing Team (2024/2025) and was recognized as the Outstanding Young Leader of the Year.

He also emerged as the Best History Quizzer in Liberia, an award presented by Educate the Next Generation in collaboration with the Ministry of Education.
